About Us

Centrally located on Vancouver Island, Schaffers’ Equipment, Truck & Trailer Repairs (2012) Ltd. is a family-owned Canadian business located at 110 Bowlsby Street, Nanaimo, B.C. and has been in business for 40 years.

 

Schaffers’ Equipment has a B620 License to inspect and test Highway Fuel Tanker Trucks and is an Associate Dealer for International Trucks. Schaffers’ has an extensive Parts Department and a full staff of experienced and knowledgeable Heavy Duty Mechanics and Welders. The Schaffers’ team provides a fully diversified service and parts operation, including motor vehicle inspections, to our many customers.

The Schaffers’ Group of Companies also includes BGS Storage  which offers Nanaimo and area a secure storage facility for trucks, trailers, RVs, cars, boats, and more.
