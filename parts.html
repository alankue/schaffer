Parts

Our experienced and dedicated team of Certified Parts Technicians enjoy helping our customers keep their business profitable by supplying quality parts at competitive prices. Schaffers’ not only carries a wide range of inventory, but also has direct access to manufacturers’ warehouses. We have parts for all makes of heavy trucks!

Schaffers’ provides direct delivery to most Vancouver Island locations and our parts team is available Monday to Friday, 7:00 am to 10:00 pm and Saturday, 7:30 am to 4:00 pm. We are closed all statutory holidays.
