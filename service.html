Service

We are a designated inspection facility, a federally licensed facility for the inspection and testing of highway and portable tanker trucks, as well as an ICBC approved repair facility. Our skilled and dedicated service specialists provide a wide and diverse range of services on all makes and models of trucks, trailers, and tanks.

Our heavy duty mechanical services include:

  • Truck & Heavy Equipment Repair
  • Engine Overhaul on Cummins & International
  • Hydraulic and air hose assembly
  • “U” bolt bending
  • Trailer suspensions
  • Custom fabricating
  • Mig & Tig welding
  • Air conditioning repairs
  • Driveline repair and fabrication

Our service team is available Monday to Friday, 7:00 am to 7:00 pm and Saturday’s 7:30 am to 4:00 pm. We also provide after hours emergency roadside assistance
